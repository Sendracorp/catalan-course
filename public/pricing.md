Verbadium sells each language course as a one-time purchase. No subscription, no recurring fees.

- Includes: 12 units, 108 interactive exercises, a full timed mock A1 exam, a 275-entry glossary with native-speaker audio, and progress sync across devices
- Free preview: Unit 1 (no account needed)
- Taught in: English, Spanish, French, Russian, German — each sold separately at €25
- Checkout: card payment via Paddle (merchant of record; tax added at checkout based on buyer location)
